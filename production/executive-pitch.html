Module 6 · Roadmap

Three phases. Each one earns the next.

Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-8)

Invite auth, contact import, structured profiles, network map, manual intro flow + push.

Phase 2: AI Layer (Weeks 9-14)

Natural language search, trust-path ranking, AI drafting, and smart result prioritization.

Phase 3: Growth + Monetization (Weeks 15-20)

Reputation scoring, gap detection, outcome tracking, and Pro/Closer monetization.

Roadmap logic: prove intro flow, then scale intelligence, then compound monetization.
